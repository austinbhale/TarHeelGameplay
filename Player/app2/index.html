Stop List
TimePrompt

Set the time for a stop point by dragging the video slider, or using the buttons below the video to advance time in steps of 0.1, 1, or 10 seconds. When you have the time you want, enter text for the Prompt and click Add.

You can update an existing stop point by clicking on it in the list to the left of the video, modifying the Time (as above) or Prompt and clicking Update.
